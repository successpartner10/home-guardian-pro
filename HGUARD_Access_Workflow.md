# HGUARD Elite — Guard Duty Workflow

This guide explains how you manage platform access and how **HGUARD ensures 100% privacy** between different users.

---

## 🔒 The Privacy Model: Total Isolation
It is important to understand that HGUARD Elite uses a **Siloed Privacy Model**:
- **Separate Accounts**: Every user who signs in has their own private dashboard.
- **Data Isolation**: User A **cannot** see User B's cameras, alerts, or recordings.
- **Private Storage**: Each user saves recordings to their **own** Google Drive. Your recordings never touch another user's storage.
- **Your Approval**: When you "Approve" a user, you are simply giving them permission to **use the HGUARD platform**. You are **NOT** giving them access to your cameras.

---

## 1. New User: The Sign-up Phase
1.  **The Link**: You send the user your invite link (found in the "Gatekeeper" section of Settings).
2.  **The Action**: The user clicks **Sign in with Google** on the login page.
3.  **The Result**: The app detects they are not on your "Approved" list. It creates their profile in the background but blocks their access.
4.  **The Waiting Room**: The user sees a "Pending Approval" screen. They cannot see cameras or recordings yet.

---

## 2. Admin: The Approval Phase (Your Job)
1.  **Login**: Log in to HGUARD Elite with your admin account (`successpartner10@gmail.com`).
2.  **Access Settings**: Click the **Gear Icon** (Settings) in the sidebar or bottom navigation.
3.  **Open Gatekeeper**:
    - Scroll down to the **Security & Mesh** section.
    - Click the button labeled **"Gatekeeper (Manage Users)"**.
4.  **Action**: 
    - You will see a card for the new user.
    - If they are new, the button will say **"PENDING"** (pulsing orange).
    - **Click the button**. It will turn blue and say **"APPROVED"**.

---

## 3. Final Result
The moment you click "Approved," the user's "Waiting Room" screen will instantly disappear, and they will be taken to the Dashboard. They now have full access to view cameras and the archive.

---

## 🚨 Revoking Access
You can revoke access at any time by going back to the Gatekeeper and clicking the **"APPROVED"** button to flip them back to **"PENDING"**.
